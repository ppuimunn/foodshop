About us

Our Beginnings

The famous Traveller began their cooking journey when they were stranded in Teyvat and needed to be self-sufficient. After saving Paimon, the Traveller began travelling all over Teyvat in search for their sibling.
In the process, the Traveller obtained many recipes from prominent chefs in Teyvat, including Chef Mao and Xiangling from Wanmin Restaurant, Brook from Mondstadt, Shimura Kanbei from Shimura's, Lambad from Lambad's Tavern and many more!
